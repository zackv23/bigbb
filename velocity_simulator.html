FICO Checking & KYC verification
FICO is pulled (usually soft, rarely hard). Your 740 score establishes premium financial stability. Under-30 age represents a normal profile, creating no flags.
Step 2 — ChexSystems Query
Recent Inquiry & Account Velocity Check
A clean profile with 0 inquiries in 24 months represents 0 risk. Under current velocity, ChexSystems reports 1 inquiry. The bank views this as normal consumer behavior. Approval rates remain high.
Step 3 — EWS Risk Scoring
Early Warning Services Multi-Bank Database
EWS checks for cross-bank account status, charge-offs, and velocity indicators. Since your history is clean, no negative file is returned. Velocity flags are not triggered.
